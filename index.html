Balance Logic
🏭 Vendor Accounts
Payments to vendors are negative balances (you are owed goods).

When vendor delivers, value is deducted from negative balance.

Goes positive if goods exceed advances.
🏢 Company Accounts
Goods supplied create a positive balance (they owe you).

When company pays, balance is reduced.

Goes negative if they overpay.
